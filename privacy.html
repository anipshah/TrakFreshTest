Privacy Policy for TrakFresh

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1. Data We Collect

  • Camera images: Only when you grant permission, used to scan receipts and food labels for expiry tracking
  • Device identifiers: For analytics and crash reporting
  • Purchase information: For subscription management via Google Play
  • App usage data: To improve our service

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide app features (scan items, track expiry dates)
  • Improve service quality and fix bugs
  • Process subscriptions and purchases
  • Send notifications about expiring items

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for analytics and RevenueCat for subscription management. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. Camera images are processed locally and not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to backup your data.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@trakfresh.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
